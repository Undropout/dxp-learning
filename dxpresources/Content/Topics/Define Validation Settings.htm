Define Validation Settings

Complete these steps to define the validation settings for your data model:

  1. In the directory carousel, click the project for which you want to define validation settings.
  2. In the directory, click the application for which you want to define validation settings.
  3. Click the Data Editor tab.
  4. Click the ADM sub-tab.
  5. To define validation settings for an array, click the Array link, and then click Validation in the right pane. The Validation area displays:

    • Click the Min/Max items slider:
      • Enter the minimum number of items into the Min field.
      • Enter the maximum number of items into the Max field.
    • Click the Character length slider:
      • Enter the minimum number of characters into the Min field.
      • Enter the maximum number of characters into the Max field.
    • Click the List slider, and then choose an available list.

    Note: Enter the applicable data into the search field to search for available lists.

    • Click the Regular Expression (regex) slider, and then choose an available expression from the drop-down list.
    • Click Save.
  6. To define validation settings for a property, click the Property link, and then click Validation in the right pane. The Validation area displays:

    • Click the Character length slider:
      • Enter the minimum number of items into the Min field.
      • Enter the maximum number of items into the Max field.
    • Click the List slider, and then choose an available list.

    Note: Enter the applicable data into the search field to search for available lists.

    • Click the Regular Expression slider, and then choose an available expression from the drop-down list.
    • Click Save.
