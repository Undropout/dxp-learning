Create a Business Feature

Complete these steps to create a business feature:

  1. In the directory carousel, click the project that contains the application for which you want to create a business feature.
  2. Hover your cursor over the application, and then click the Menu icon.

  1. Click the Add icon, and the Add Feature box displays:

  1. Enter a name for the new business feature into the Feature field. The name must be between 5 and 35 characters in length and must contain only letters, numbers, and other approved characters.

Note: Blank spaces are not allowed.

  1. Click Save.
