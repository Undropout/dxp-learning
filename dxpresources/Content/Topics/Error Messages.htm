Error Messages

One of the main ways that Journey Creator alerts you about errors is via the Cancel/Save banner that displays in the lower area of the environment while creating or editing your data. When errors are present, the word Error displays in the middle of the banner. To view the error, simply hover your cursor over the word, and any errors will appear in a pop-out message format.
