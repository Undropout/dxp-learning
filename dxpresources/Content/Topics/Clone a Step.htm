Clone a Step

Use the following procedure to clone an existing step:

  1. In the directory carousel, click the project that contains the application, business feature, and step that you want to clone.
  2. In the directory, click the application, business feature, and then the step that you want to clone. 
  3. Hover your cursor over the step and click the Menu icon:

  1. Click the Clone icon, and the Clone Step window displays:

  1. Update the following fields in the Clone Step window:
    • Select the business feature to which you want to add the cloned step from the Feature drop-down menu.
    • Enter a name for the cloned step into the Step field. The name must be between 5 and 35 characters in length and must contain only letters, numbers, and other approved characters.

    Note: Blank spaces are not allowed.

    • If desired, select a template for the cloned step from the Template drop-down menu.
  2. Click Clone.
